## SEO Best Practices Applied

✅ **Length:** 150-160 characters (optimal for Google)
✅ **Keywords:** Include primary keywords naturally
✅ **Features:** Mention specific product features (RFID, water-resistant)
✅ **Benefits:** Highlight user benefits (protection, personalization)
✅ **Call-to-Action:** Include action words (shop, create, browse)
✅ **Target Audience:** Mention who the product is for (travelers, business travelers)
✅ **Unique Value:** Emphasize customization and personalization
